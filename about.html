Household: The household population of Barangay I-B in the 2015 Census was 4,260, broken down into 1,039 households, or an average of 4.10 members per household.

Population by Age Group: According to the 2015 Census, the age group with the highest population in Barangay I-B is 5 to 9, with 405 individuals. Conversely, the age group with the lowest population is 80 and over, with 56 individuals.

Details:

Combining age groups together, those aged 14 and below make up 26.69% (1,137) of the population. Those aged 15 to 64 constitute 66.29% (2,824), and senior citizens aged 65 and over total 7.02% (299).

The computed Age Dependency Ratios indicate that there are 40 youth dependents to every 100 working-age individuals, 11 senior citizens to every 100 working individuals, and overall, 51 dependents to every 100 working individuals.

The median age of 28 indicates that half of the population of Barangay I-B are aged less than 28 and the other half are over the age of 28.
